Who We Are
Empowering communities since 2020 — rooted in transparency and compassion

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act 2013, licensed by the Central Government, and based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. Founded on 23rd September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), the foundation works tirelessly for societal betterment through inclusive projects.

We are 80G & 12A certified, CSR-1 registered, NITI Aayog (NGO Darpan) recognized, and IAF ISO 9001:2015 certified — a testament to high-quality management and donor trust. Our mission: to foster lasting change by bridging gaps in food security, education, health, environment, and economic opportunities.

Our Flagship Projects
Holistic initiatives driving measurable impact on ground — as featured in our official report

Project Seva

Daily meal distribution in slums, dry ration kits to low-income families, winter clothes & blankets for homeless. Over 50,000+ meals served and COVID-19 relief across states.

🍲 50,000+ meals distributed

Bachpanshala

Sponsoring education for underprivileged children: books, uniforms, school fees. After-school mentorship & health check-ups to break the poverty cycle.

📚 1000+ children reached

Project Jeev

Animal welfare: feeding 50+ stray animals daily, rescue coordination, medical aid, and community awareness for compassion towards street animals.

🐾 50+ animals fed daily

Project Udaan

Digital campaigns & skill development for women & girls. Entrepreneurship workshops, menstrual health awareness, 900+ girls empowered with financial literacy.

💪 900+ women empowered

Project Prakriti

Plastic-free campaigns, tree plantations (20,000+ trees), waste segregation units, and promoting cloth/jute bags to build sustainable communities.

🌳 20,000+ trees planted

Project Vikas

Vocational training for youth & women, soft skills, computer literacy, and livelihood enhancement — creating sustainable income opportunities.

👩‍💻 500+ youth trained
